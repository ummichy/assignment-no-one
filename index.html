Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Leveraging advanced technologies, creative solutions, and innovative approaches to enhance the economy and improve the quality of life.

Sustainability

Emphasizing eco-friendly practices, efficient resource utilization, and long-term strategies to secure a sustainable future for coming generations.

Infrastructure

Developing modern transportation, communication systems, and digital networks to strengthen connectivity and foster national growth.

Empowerment

Advancing education, skill development, and employment opportunities for women, youth, and marginalized groups to foster equity and social progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Promoting renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and biogas to ensure environmental sustainability.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Technology can create opportunities for the poorest of the poor, connecting them to education, healthcare, and financial services. It is a powerful tool to eliminate inequality and bridge the urban-rural divide.Renewable energy is the future. Solar power, biogas, and wind energy will replace fossil fuels, making energy accessible to everyone while protecting the environment.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
